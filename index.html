Hey, I'm Shreya!

Fueled by high energy levels and boundless enthusiasm, I’m easily inspired and more then willing to follow my fascinations wherever they take me. When not coding, you'll find me surfing the internet for Tech Blogs or neck deep in a thriller fiction. I often indulge in baking and trying my hand out at competitive programming.
